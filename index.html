Reflection

As I reflect on my experience of creating my personal portfolio website. The errors I encountered, such as aligning the skills icon as a gallery feature and adding JavaScript code that suited the code, pushed me to think critically and creatively to solve these problems. Through this experience, I gained a deeper appreciation for the work that web developers do. It was fascinating to see how they solve complex issues and create new web pages from scratch. I also learned how important it is to customize templates to suit the specific needs of the project, rather than just using them as-is. Working with the template allowed me to focus more on the content of my portfolio, rather than getting bogged down in the design and technical aspects. It was a time-saving solution that helped me create a functional and visually appealing website in a relatively short amount of time. Overall, I'm proud of what I accomplished with my portfolio website and the valuable lessons I learned along the way. I'm excited to continue honing my web development skills and exploring new ways to showcase my work in the future.
